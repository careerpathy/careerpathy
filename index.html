Our Mission

At Careerpathy, our mission is to empower early career software engineers and career switchers by providing comprehensive career coaching and mentoring services. We are dedicated to equipping individuals with the knowledge, guidance, and confidence they need to excel in the tech industry. Through our personalized approach, we offer tailored tips, advice, and support, enabling our clients to navigate the challenges of transitioning into tech, enhance their technical skills, overcome imposter syndrome, and maintain a consistent learning mindset.

About Me

I'm Chislaine Cruz, founder of Careerpathy. As a former bootcamp student and teaching fellow, I am incredibly passionate about teaching others how to code and helping them achieve a fulfilling and rewarding career in the tech industry.

Since 2021, I have been working as a Fullstack Software Engineer, starting my journey as an apprentice and gradually transitioning into a full-time engineering role. Throughout my career, I have gained invaluable experiences and insights that have shaped my understanding of the industry.

Driven by the desire to share my knowledge and experiences, I wrote a couple of articles detailing my personal journey as a bootcamp graduate transitioning into my first full-time job. These resonated with many aspiring software engineers who reached out to me seeking guidance and support.

At Careerpathy, I am dedicated to providing the guidance and support that individuals need to navigate the complex world of tech. I am here to help you kickstart your journey, build your skills, and become the best version of yourself.
